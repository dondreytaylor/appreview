Why use AppReview?

Getting a lot of reviews and ratings in the App Store is the goal of anyone with one or many apps in the App Store, but getting them can be difficult. The problem is two-fold-- first, it can be a daunting task to get showcased, with over 1.5 million apps in the App Store and over 500 being added each day; this makes visibility an uphill battle. Second, there is really not much incentive for app users to review apps. While most people review apps to request features or recommend an app to others, the large majority of people who leave reviews are the ones that have had a bad experience-- that's where AppReview comes in!

AppReview sets out to provide a plateform for both app reviewers and creators, by bringing app reviewers to apps that need reviews through incentives.
